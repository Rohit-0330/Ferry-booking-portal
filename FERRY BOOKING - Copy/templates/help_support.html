Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I book a ferry?

To book a ferry, use our booking form on the homepage. Enter your departure and arrival ports, select your travel dates, and choose the ferry that best suits your needs. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or change your booking through the "Manage My Booking" section on our website. Please note that cancellations and changes may be subject to fees depending on the ferry operator's policies.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards and PayPal. Ensure that your payment method is valid and has sufficient funds.
